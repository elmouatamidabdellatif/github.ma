Biography

Elmouatamid Abdellatif is a Junior Professor Chair at the University of French Guiana, France. He was a Postdoctoral research associate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, New Jersey Institute of Technology, USA (2022-2024). He was in a postdoctoral position at IRESEN (Institute of Research in Solar Energy and New Energies), Morocco (2021-2022). He received a Ph.D. in Energy Engineering and Automation from Chouaib Doukkali University of El-Jadida, Morocco (Dec-2020). He received a Master from Chouaib Doukkali University with 1st class honors (2016). He has research experience in transmission and distribution lines, power systems, energy efficiency in buildings, smart microgrids, energy management, anomaly detection, and predictive maintenance, and engaged in numerous collaborative research projects.
